Profile

Approach

Player coach by nature

A player coach by nature, I've held Principal roles leading complex, cross-functional projects from discovery to delivery. In these settings I lean on combinations of research and service design and human centred design to provide rigour and a firm focus on customer value.

On the human side, I've helped mentor and lead designers and researchers. This includes fostering mentoring individual contributors, fostering communities of practice and most recently exploring how AI tools can be used to accelerate and amplify teams without compromising on rigour or quality.
